About Us

At Encontre Me, we are dedicated to reuniting families and loved ones by providing a centralized platform for sharing information about missing persons. Our mission is to create a community-driven space where people can collaborate, share, and assist in the search for missing individuals, ensuring that no one has to go through this alone.

Built on values of empathy, security, and transparency, our platform is designed to be safe, accessible, and effective. We believe in the power of collaboration and strive to make every search effort more impactful, bringing hope and support to those in need.

Why choosing Us?

For Instituitions

We offer a reliable and organized solution for managing and publicizing multiple cases simultaneously. Our platform ensures secure data handling and streamlined case management, making it easier for institutions to focus on what truly matters—finding the missing.

For people

Our user-friendly interface allows families to quickly and easily share information about their missing loved ones. We prioritize the privacy and safety of your data, ensuring that you can focus on what matters most—bringing your loved ones back home.

What About the Crisis?

Sao Paulo 62 Flight deaths

In the aftermath of the São Paulo G2 Flight Disasters, a devastating event that shook the entire nation, the power of community and technology came to the forefront. Families were torn apart, and the urgency to find and identify missing individuals was paramount. Amid the chaos, our platform played a pivotal role in the search and rescue efforts. Through our centralized system, families, volunteers, and rescue teams were able to quickly share crucial information, including descriptions, last-known locations, and identifying details. This collaborative approach enabled a faster, more organized response, which was essential in those critical hours and days following the disaster. Our platform's ability to facilitate real-time updates and streamline communication between different parties was instrumental in reuniting families with their loved ones. This tragic event highlighted the true potential of our platform in making a difference when it matters most.
